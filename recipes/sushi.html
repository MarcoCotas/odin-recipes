Pastel De Nata Recipe

How to make your own perfect pastel de nata

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 1 lemon peel
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 package puff pastry

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a saucepan, combine sugar, milk, flour, egg yolks, lemon peel, and cinnamon stick. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until thickened.
  3. Remove from heat and let cool slightly. Remove lemon peel and cinnamon stick.
  4. Roll out puff pastry and cut into circles to fit muffin tin cups. Press pastry into cups.
  5. Fill each cup with the custard mixture.
  6. Bake in preheated oven until pastry is golden brown and custard is set, about 20 minutes.
  7. Let cool before serving.
